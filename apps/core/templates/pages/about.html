PHOSPHORUS

Phosphorus is an app for sun-based mindfulness. A Kickstart Coding Back-end Project.

Get a reminder 30 minutes before sunrise or sunset to have a chance to get outside and enjoy the sun. Watching a sunrise/sunset has noted mental benefits, and is a great way to start or end your day.

Phosphorus utilizes HereMaps and AirVisual APIs to calculate geo-specific data based on user input and IP address.
