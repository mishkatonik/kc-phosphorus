PHOSPHORUS

Phosphorus is an app for sun-based mindfulness. Get a reminder 30 minutes before sunrise and sunset to have a chance to get outside and enjoy the sun. Watching a sunrise/sunset has noted mental benefits, and is a great way to start or end your day. The app combines sun-mindfulness with positive affirmations for meditation and stress-relief.
